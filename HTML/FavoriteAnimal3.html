History
Snow leopards are found at altitudes between 9,800 and 17,000 feet in the high, rugged mountains of Central Asia. Their range spans from Afghanistan to Kazakstan and Russia in the north to India and China in the east. China contains about 60% of snow leopard habitat. Snow Leopards could live their whole live in solitude and could never come across humans or antoher snow Leopard its whole life. They eat anything from birds to bugs.
